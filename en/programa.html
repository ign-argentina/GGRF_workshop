Program

The workshop will be held in English from Monday, Sep 16th to Friday, Sep 20th, 2019. The preliminary schedule considers four slots per day with two coffee breaks of 30 min each one and a lunch break of two hours. A technical visit to AGGO is planned for Friday's afternoon.

The planned sessions are:

Opening session

  • • Welcome
  • • Workshop objectives
  • • Geodesy of reference in Latin America: status, challenges, expected learnings from the workshop (SIRGAS executive committee + gravity community)

GGRF definition and organization

  • • United Nations Initiative on Global Geospatial Information Management (UN-GGIM): Mission, vision, structure, current projects.
  • • The UN Resolution on the GGRF: objectives, implications and new opportunities to improve geodetic infrastructure.
  • • IAG vision on the implementation of the GGRF resolution
  • • UN-GGIM Regional Committee for the Americas (UN-GGIM Americas): Mission, vision, structure, current projects, how to homogenize the participation of all Latin American countries, how can UN-GGIM Americas contribute to improve the geodetic infrastructure in Latin America.
  • • UN-GGIM Americas and SIRGAS: which role should play SIRGAS in UN-GGIM Americas, how can SIRGAS improve its participation in and support to UN-GGIM Americas.
  • • UN-GGIM Subcommittee on Geodesy: Mission, vision, structure, current projects, participation of Latin America, how to improve the participation of Latin America, how can Latin America contribute to and profit from the UN Subcommittee on Geodesy.
  • • The GGRF roadmap
  • • GGRF Governance
  • • Infrastructure for the GGRF
