Traveler Services

Practical suggestions

Electricity: Argentina's electric power supply has 220 volts and 50 Hz. The sockets in Argentina have 2 cylindrical holes (type C / E / F) or 3 flat holes (type I).

Time zone: The time zone in Argentina corresponds to GMT-3 and is currently not modified during the summer months.

Trade hours: Shops in Buenos Aires are open Monday through Friday from 9 am to 8 pm and Saturdays from 9 am to 1 pm, although the shops of the main avenues remain open throughout the afternoon. In shopping malls, the schedule extends to 10 pm even on weekends and holidays.

Bank Schedules: Banks in Buenos Aires open their doors at 10 am, and close them at 3 pm. Cash withdrawals and other transactions can be made at ATMs, which operate 24 hours a day.

Local currency: The Argentine currency is the peso (AR $). There are notes/bills of $1000, $500, $200, $100, $50, $20, $10, $5 and coins of $10, $5, $2, $1, 50 cents, 25 cents and 10 cents.

Exchange rate: The US dollar is the most widely used international currency. It can be exchanged in Banks or Exchange Houses, by showing your passport. The official exchange rate can be consulted in the following websites: Central Bank of the Argentine Republic or National Bank of Argentina.

Means of transport: The means of public transport that can be used to get to the venue are the following (you can get the "BA Cómo llego" app for your mobile phone):

  • - Buses: 41-57-67-68-152-161-194
  • - Subway/Underground: D line, Olleros or Carranza stations
  • - Train lines: Bartolomé Mitre line (Carranza station) and José León Suarez line (Carranza station)
  • - Taxi: (you can get the "BA Taxi" app for your mobile phone)

Visa procedures

Tourist visa applications may be submitted to any Consular Office of the Argentine Republic. The authorized stay period for this type of visa is a maximum of ninety (90) days. Applications are personal and the applicant must submit the following documents (an original and a copy) at the Consular Office. For more information about the procedure please visit the following web page

Visit Buenos Aires / Visit Argentina

Argentina offers a wide variety of tourist destinations: vibrant cities, tropical jungles, amazing glaciers, alpine forests and even the first glimpse of Antarctica in the south. The variety of landscapes in Argentina and its cultural diversity is unique and has been a tourist attraction for decades.

For information about the tourist options that Argentina offers please visit the following websites: turismo.buenosaires.gob.ar www.argentina.gob.ar/turismo/turista
